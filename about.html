About Scribsy

Scribsy is an anonymous, digital “freedom wall” where anyone can share short text rants or quick doodles—no account needed, no judgement allowed. Every post carries an optional mood tag (“Happy”, “Dreamy”, “Meh”) and the name you choose (or “Anonymous”). Each day the wall resets, but archives are forever saved in Past Walls for you to revisit.

Our mission is to give you a safe, fun space to express yourself freely, uncluttered by logins, teams, or trolls.

How It Works

  1. Click “What’s on your mind?” to open the post modal.
  2. Choose to Write (up to 100 characters) or Draw (quick doodles).
  3. Optionally add your name & a mood tag.
  4. Your post appears instantly in the masonry wall.
  5. Every 24 hours the wall wipes clean—and a snapshot goes to Past Walls.

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful. No hate speech, harassment, or threats.
  • Keep it legal. No content that’s violent, illegal, or infringes on others’ rights.
  • No personal info. Don’t share private data about yourself or others.
  • Stay on topic. Posts should be your thoughts, rants, or doodles—no spam or self-promotion.
  • Moderation. Admins can remove posts that break these rules (Ctrl + Shift + A to enter Admin Mode).

Terms & Privacy

Data storage: All posts and archives live in your browser’s localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on our servers. Clearing your browser storage will delete everything.

Cookies & tracking: Scribsy does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party trackers. Your activity stays on your device and never leaves.

Disclaimer: Use Scribsy at your own risk. We’re not responsible for any content you or others post. By using Scribsy you agree to abide by these guidelines.
