About Scribsy

Scribsy is your digital freedom wall — a space to express thoughts and emotions anonymously. Whether you're writing from the heart, doodling your imagination, or simply vibing with your mood, Scribsy lets you share without judgment.

Inspired by real-world “Freedom Walls,” Scribsy keeps things simple, raw, and real.

🧠 No accounts.
🖊️ Just thoughts.
🎨 Just scribbles.
💬 Just you.

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful – No hate speech, harassment, or bullying.
  • No NSFW or offensive content – This is a shared space.
  • No spam or self-promotion – Keep it real and meaningful.
  • Keep it anonymous – No personal info or doxxing.
  • Posts reset daily – What you write today will be gone tomorrow. Let go.

⚠️ Inappropriate posts may be removed by the admin without warning.

Why Scribsy Exists

In a world of curated perfection and endless judgment, we wanted to bring back something honest — a space to just write and draw freely. No likes. No followers. No pressure. Just... Scribsy.

Let's keep Scribsy weird, wonderful, and welcoming. 🎨
